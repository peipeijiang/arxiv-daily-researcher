<summary><strong>中文摘要</strong></summary>

在技术与可持续发展快速演变的背景下，利用推荐系统（Recommender Systems）已成为推动积极变革的有力工具。以人工智能（AI）和数据分析为基础，推荐系统可在从电子商务到能源管理、包容性与福祉等多个领域发挥有效作用。通过在多利益相关方视角下运用推荐算法的力量，组织与研究人员能够引导用户做出更可持续的选择与行为，从而助力实现更广泛的环境与社会目标。为此，本研讨会为研究者、实践者及平台所有者提供了一个独特的平台，共同探索将可持续发展原则融入推荐系统的路径。通过主题报告、讨论与专题座谈，参与者可深入探讨可持续推荐系统的理论基础、实践应用以及伦理与环境考量。本研讨会旨在促进合作与知识交流，激发创新，并推动集体行动，共同迈向更加可持续的未来。

<summary><strong>英文摘要</strong></summary>

In the rapidly evolving landscape of technology and sustainability, leveraging Recommender Systems has emerged as a powerful tool for driving positive change. With a foundation in AI and data analytics, Recommender Systems can be effective in various domains, from e-commerce to energy management, inclusion and well-being. By harnessing the power of recommendation algorithms under a multi-stakeholder perspective, organizations and researchers can guide users towards more sustainable choices and behaviors, contributing to broader environmental and social goals. With this aim, our workshop provides a unique platform for researchers, practitioners, and platform owners to explore the integration of sustainability principles into Recommender Systems. Through presentations, discussions, and panels, participants can explore the theoretical foundations, practical implementations, and ethical and environmental considerations of sustainable Recommender Systems. By fostering collaboration and knowledge exchange, the workshop aims to catalyze innovation and inspire collective action towards a more sustainable future.
