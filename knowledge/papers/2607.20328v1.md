<summary><strong>中文摘要</strong></summary>

本研究对生成式人工智能作为学术图书馆资源的新兴发现路径进行了实证分析。利用2023年8月至2025年10月的网络分析数据，研究发现由人工智能中介的流量显著增加，尤其是在集成链接引用功能之后。引荐来源分析识别出ChatGPT、Perplexity和Gemini是驱动该流量的主要平台。相当一部分用户访问了机构知识库，主要获取电子学位论文。这一模式表明，人工智能检索机制能够有效呈现那些具有结构化元数据、稳定永久链接且为开放获取（Open Access）并免费可用的资源。研究结果揭示了当前人工智能生态系统如何展示图书馆资源，并强调了针对不断演变的人工智能格局进行持续分析与战略回应的必要性。

<summary><strong>英文摘要</strong></summary>

This study empirically analyzed generative AI as an emerging discovery pathway to academic library resources. Utilizing web analytics from August 2023 to October 2025, the research identifies a significant increase in AI-mediated traffic, particularly following the integration of linked citation features. Referral analysis identified ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Gemini as the primary platforms driving this traffic. A substantial portion of users reached the institutional repository, primarily accessing electronic theses and dissertations. This pattern suggests that AI retrieval mechanisms effectively surface resources with structured metadata and stable permalinks that are Open Access and freely available. The results illustrate how AI ecosystems currently expose library resources and underscore the need for continued analysis and a strategic response to the evolving AI landscape.
